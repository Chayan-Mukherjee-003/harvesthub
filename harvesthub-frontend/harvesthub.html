Reaping Fair Rewards: The Direct Path from Farm to Shelf.

Harvest Hub is a digital platform creating a direct, transparent, and secure connection between farmers and retailers.

I'm a Farmer I'm a Retailer

Why Do Our Farmers Struggle?

Agriculture remains the backbone of India’s economy, yet the very farmers who feed our nation often struggle to earn fair profits due to middlemen who take a large portion of their earnings.

Our Solution: A Fairer Marketplace

Harvest Hub addresses this challenge by creating a digital marketplace that empowers farmers and provides reliability to retailers, building a more sustainable future for Indian agriculture.

Simple, Secure, and Transparent

Follow these four simple steps to get started on our platform.

1: Verified Registration

Farmers and retailers register with official IDs (Kisan Card, FSSAI) to ensure a trusted community.

2: Place or Receive Orders

Retailers buy ready crops or pre-order future harvests directly from local, verified farmers.

3: Secure Advance Payments

For pre-orders, retailers pay a 20% advance, giving farmers capital while securing the order.

4: Local Delivery & Final Payment

Trade is within a 200 km radius. Final payment is completed upon successful delivery.

Built on Trust and Innovation

Financial Security

Our unique payment system protects everyone with fair risk-sharing in case of unforeseen crop failures.

Verified Community

Every user is verified. A two-way rating system builds transparency and rewards professionalism.

Hyperlocal Sourcing

Our 200 km radius reduces costs, supports local economies, and ensures the freshest produce.
